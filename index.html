I build the systems behind the scenes — the ones that make events run, queues disappear, and 300+ people move through a room without chaos.

I'm a B.Com (Hons.) student at Delhi University, but most of my real education has happened in the field — as Operations Head of my college's technical society, coordinating live events, and as a self-taught developer building the QR systems and websites that power them.

My work sits at the intersection of business operations and digital execution: financial accounting and Tally ERP on one side, HTML/CSS/JavaScript and deployed web systems on the other. I like being the person who can plan the workflow and build the tool that runs it.
